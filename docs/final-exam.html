13.1 Important Instructions

  1. The final exam consists of three parts. There are 9 exercises (and one bonus activity) in total .

    • Part I is about the use of control structures;
    • Part II focuses on regular expressions;
    • Part III analyzes a real dataset of Mandarin pop songs.

  2. The total grade of the exam is 100%. Exercises 13.1 to 13.8 are 10% each; Exercise 13.9 is 20%. (The bonus activity is 20%.)

  3. The exam requires two datasets. Please make sure that all necessary files have been properly downloaded from Drobpox Drive and saved under the demo_data folder of your working directory.

    • demo_data/data-chinese-songs-mojim-exam.csv
    • demo_data/data-sentences.txt

  4. Your submitted R script should follow the exact same format as your previously submitted assignments. Namely, you need to indicate clearly which parts of the codes respond to which exam question. Always specify clearly the exercise number. Codes without clear structured comments indicating the number of exercises will be ignored.

  5. Please note that your submitted script will be evaluated by sourcing the entire script in the working directory (i.e., a directory with the demo_data folder containing all necessary data files). And the performance of your scripts will be assessed in terms of how close the outputs are to the sample results provided after each exercise.

  6. Please submit your R script by: 13:20, Fri., June 19, 2020 via Moodle.

  7. Name your script as follows: final-alvin.R (use your own name in the filename).

  8. You must write codes on your own. Asking others to write for you is absolutely forbidden. Any report of misconduct will be taken seriously.
